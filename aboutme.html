    • As you probably know, My name is Mauricio and I am based in Austin, TX. Currently, I feel very comfortable working with React, Next.js, and writing my own CSS, but I am not tied to any of these. I also love exploring different technologies and have found many interesting ones. Recently, I started working with p5.js, ChakraUI, Vite, and others.

    About two years ago, I completed the Coding Bootcamp from the University of Texas. It was an awesome experience where I got to learn JavaScript, Node.js, MySQL, MongoDB, React, and more! But my coding journey did not begin there...

    (only if you're very interested)

    I started with a dream of making a game, so I started researching and learning a bit of code. I tried Unity and Godot, and to my surprise... Making a game is extremely hard. I created a few projects, but ultimately failed. However, coding led me to the idea of creating high value design. In my mind, everyone can draw a tree but not everyone can code a tree, it might be silly (and wrong) but that is how I saw it. I saw design as a hobby not a career.

    I have always been interesting in art and design, ever since I was little, I loved drawing which translated to photoshop and illustrator later on in my life but, as I mentioned before, I never saw it as a career. This was a mistake because I honestly believe that I would have crushed design school.

    After failing at game development, I decided to join the Coding Bootcamp at UT where you can learn how to be a Full-Stack Developer in 24 weeks! This course was very challenging and I definitely had to learn way more on top of it, but without I couldn't have found my passion for creative coding, web devolpment and web design.
